The 90-Day Process

Weeks 0-2

Discovery

Goals, constraints, success metrics. Analytics sanity check, baselines, funnel map.

Weeks 3-4

Design

Opportunity scoring (impact × effort). Experiment backlog and measurement plan.

Weeks 5-10

Deliver

Ship 1–2 improvements per week. Read, learn, iterate.

Weeks 11-12

Prove

Results rollup, before/after graphs. What’s next plan (keep, kill, double-down).

Cadence: weekly standup, async Slack/Email, shared dashboard.
